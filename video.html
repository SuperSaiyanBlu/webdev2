Goku Vs The Source Of Evil

This video was made for an assignment back in Semester 1. It's a stop-motion computer animation using "sprites". I found some basic sprites from Dragonball Z online, remade them using Microsoft Paint, and placed them onto a custom made background that I made in Adobe Photoshop. The frames are individual images that were then imported into iMovie and made into a flowing animation. The sound clips and such were pasted in during this time too. It's a rudimentary video, but I still look back on it fondly!
